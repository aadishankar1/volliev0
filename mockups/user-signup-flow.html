Ocean Conservation Society

Ocean Conservation Society is dedicated to protecting marine ecosystems through education, research, and community action. We work with local communities to promote sustainable practices and organize regular cleanup initiatives.

About this Initiative

Join us for our monthly beach cleanup event! We'll be removing trash and debris from Santa Monica Beach to protect marine life and keep our beaches beautiful. All cleaning supplies will be provided. Please wear comfortable clothes and sun protection.
